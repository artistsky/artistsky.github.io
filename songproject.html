Song

Sarah Bellefeuille

Projects   About   Motion

Purpose: The objective of this project is to discover new relationships between typographic form, space, and structure. I had to convey a song through design using only a few words from the song itself. By integrating the words with the design it had to flow nicely and I wanted to encompass all of the different sounds one heard in the song. So by doing that I listened to the song on repeat over and over at least 40+ times if not more. It was about capturing the rhythm and atmosphere/feeling the song gives to the audience and being able to effectively convey it. Song reference: Shura - What's It Gonna Be? https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nJ4uBdmnKds
