Objective: Choose a positive maxim or proverb that you admire or rings true for you. This maxim must be meaningful to you and could be described as "words to live by." You should be able to clearly communicate why this maxim is meaningful to you. Although you may start with a literal interpretation, you must move away from a literal interpretation of your maxim / mantra. This typographical mantra will be the main visual element in your poster. Building upon what we learned with purposeful play during the first project, you must include some unusual material in your poster that is visibly apparent within the final version.
